About Me

I am a JavaScript Software Developer who has also worked as as school psychologist, psychometrist, and educational specialist. I grew up in Yorba Linda, California and then moved to San Diego where I got my BA in Psychology and minored in Visual Art at University of California, San Diego. I then received my MA in Educational Psychology from Chapman University. My husband and I have now lived in Seattle for at least fifteen years where I get the rain and trees I love so much! Besides writing code and administering psychoeducational and neuropsychological test, I also enjoy acting, singing, swimming, studying Ayurveda, making balloon animals, and listening to my husband play really large saxophones.
